Who We Are?



Leads is a group coaching system located in the biggest city of Pakistan, Karachi. We at Leads provides quality education in affordable fee.
The purpose of Leads is to provide a result oriented and concept based classes.
We have programs at Leads such as Matric, Inter and B. COM, we are also providing tuition classes for the students of class I to VIII.
Keeping in mind the challenges students face in their higher education, we have also introduce Computer and English courses.
We believe that the Learners of today are the leaders of tomorrow and we are putting all our efforts to produce the best leaders for our beloved country Pakistan.

Mssion & Vision


Mission

Strive to educate, assist and train children from dynamic backgrounds to achieve the best opportunities available in our Country, Pakistan.


Vision

Leads will be a top-tier educational institute, symbol of innovations and pride, supporting our modernized century by being the cornerstone of producing effective and efficient workforce with the ability to avail the dynamic economic opportunities.
